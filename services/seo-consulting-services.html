Comprehensive SEO Audit

I can complete an audit of your website to surface key SEO problems. The audit I complete will answer the following questions:

  1. Crawling + Indexing Audit: does your website have the right SEO foundation enabling Google to crawl and index your pages?
  2. Keyword + Content Match Audit: does your website offer content matching the keyword intent(s) searched by your target users enabling you to be eligible for their search results?
